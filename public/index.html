How To Play?

Welcome to Placeit. A community based pixel game!

PlaceIt is a clash of communities game where communities fight their way to win Pool Prize.

Placeit allows you to buy any pixel on 1000 x 1000 pixels canvas. Simply select the pixels with color you want in them and see your cart to buy with TRON (trx).

Each pixel price is 10 TRX and even if you want to grab other users pixels then there is no extra price involved. This has been done to give all players equal chance of grabbing as many pixels as they can.

Our player should join a community to play game properly. You can also create your own community and bring new users to join the community.

Placeit offers an equal chance of winning for all communities. To win the pool money any community must own atleast 100 x 100 pixels on canvas with a definite logo or flag of community or any design must be drawn. Once you have drawn your image you must protect it till the end of the game time from other communities.

Winners will be picked on daily basis at GMT 12:00 PM. To get a winner total pool money must reach a total of 7M TRX.

Pool money is the income from all the pixels sold on the canvas from last distribution of dividend.

Dividend or prize money is the amount of pool which is given to members of the winning community. 70% of total pool money will be distributed among the winning community members as per their share in total pixels owned by community. To be among winning members in a community, members must hold minimum 500 PLACE tokens.
